Audience

Sunshine Weather aims to help those who need sunshine, or mostly sunshine, to plan their outdoor activities. From the party planner, to the outside adventurer, to the Mom who just wants to be outside with her kids, Sunshine Weather provides a quick view into when sunshine can be expected and how long preciptation (rain or snow) will last. It also gives an overview of temperatures to help those venturing outside to plan their wardrobes ahead of time.

Personas

Jordon - The Rock Climber

Age: 35
Jordon checks Sunshine Weather early in his work week to plan his climbs for the weekend. He wishes to know if there will be sunshine so he can climb outside, or if he will need to plan on climbing inside due to precipitation.
Site Access: Mobile phone


Molly - The Outside-Loving Parent

Age: 27
Molly takes her small children outside as often as she can to explore and discover. She most loves to venture out when the sun is shining, so she looks at Sunshine Weather for a quick view of the sunshine expected in the daily and weekly forecast. She also uses it to plan for what she may need to bring to take care of her small children, i.e. sunscreen, raincoats, snow gear, etc...
Site Access: Mobile Phone and Personal Computer

Scenarios

  • Will there be sunshine for my backpacking trip this weekend?
  • How many days will the rain/snow last?
  • What weather should I prepare for in camping this week?
  • How soon will I be needing my snow gear this winter?
  • Will there be sunshine or cloud cover while my children play at the park this morning?
